Section 01

Big Picture — What This Chapter Is Really About

Bengal Partition (1905) is the pivot point of Indian nationalism. Before it: scattered regional efforts. After it: mass national movements — Swadeshi, Boycott, revolutionary orgs — and the eventual birth of Muslim League. It tells the story of how one British blunder lit the entire nationalist fire.

Chain reaction: Curzon's Partition → mass protests → Swadeshi/Boycott → Congress splits (Surat 1907) → Morley-Minto reforms 1909 → Delhi Durbar 1911 → Annulment.

🔥
Why British did it

Official reason: administrative convenience. Real reason: divide-and-rule, break Bengal's nationalist unity.

⚡
What it triggered

Swadeshi Movement, Boycott, National Education, Congress split, revolutionary secret societies.

🏛
How it ended

Annulled in 1911 at Delhi Durbar (Hardinge II's era) — British admitted defeat; capital shifted to Delhi.

🚪 Room 1 — The Governor's Office (Curzon's desk)
You see Curzon signing papers at his desk. He signs 4 acts first: Corporation Act (1899), Official Secrets Act (1904), Universities Act (1904). Then the big one: Bengal Partition — Announced July 1905, Enforced Oct 1905. His knife cuts the map in two: East (Muslims + Assam) and West (Hindus + Bihar + Orissa).

Gokhale chairs 1905 session → anti-partition approved. Dadabhai Naoroji chairs 1906 → 4 resolutions (Swaraj, Swadeshi, Boycott, Nat. Ed.). Then in Surat 1907 (Raj Ras Bihari Ghosh presides) the hall breaks in two: Moderates on left (Gokhale, Mehta) vs Extremists on right (Lal-Bal-Pal).
🏭 Room 3 — The Swadeshi Factory
Chidambaram Pillai runs a ship (Swadeshi Steam Navigation Co.) in Madras. Syed Haider Raza operates from Delhi. Lajpat Rai fuels Punjab. Tilak hosts Ganpati festival in Pune. Valentine Chirol stands at the gate with a sign: "Father of Indian Unrest."

Walk downstairs: Vasudev Phadke's Ramosi (1879) → Chapekar brothers shooting plague commissioner (1897) → Savarkar's Mitra Mela → Abhinav Bharat (1904) → Anushilan Samiti (1902 Bengal) → India House in London (Shyamji Krishna Verma, 1905) → Alipore Bomb Case (1908) → Gadar Party in San Francisco (1913) → Komagata Maru ship (1914).
🕌 Room 5 — The Durbar Tent (1911 Delhi)
King George V sits on throne. Hardinge II announces: "Bengal is reunited! Capital moves from Calcutta to Delhi!" Muslims get appeased because Muslim League was born in 1906 (Dhaka, Aga Khan + Nawab Salimullah). Capital officially moves in 1912.

📌 Why Was Bengal Partitioned?

Official British Reason

Administrative convenience — Bengal housed 1/4th of British India's population, too large to govern efficiently.

Real Reason

Bengal was the nerve centre of nationalist/anti-British activity. Divide it along Hindu–Muslim lines = weaken united resistance.

East Bengal (Muslim-majority) + Assam | West Bengal + Bihar + Orissa (Hindu-majority)

Section 04

Smart Memory Hooks

🔥 Hook — The 4 Resolutions of 1906

Imagine a Shopping Bill (S-S-B-N): You went shopping and bought Sweater (Swaraj), Shoes (Swadeshi), a Bag (Boycott), and a Notepad (National Education). Four items, one bill. That's the 1906 Calcutta session.

🔥 Hook — Lal-Bal-Pal Trio

Three colours of a lal-gul-pal (red-yellow-green traffic light): Lala Lajpat Rai (Punjab), Bal Gangadhar Tilak (Maharashtra), Pal Bipin Chandra Pal (Bengal). Traffic was stopped for the British!

⭐ Hook — Dadabhai's 3 Sessions

Dadabhai Naoroji presided Congress 3 times: 1886, 1893, 1906. Remember: 7-year intervals. 86 → 93 (7 yrs) → 06 (13 yrs with 1906 being the Calcutta landmark). Or: "Old man returns every few years."

⭐ Hook — Tilak's Two Papers

Maratha speaks English, Kesari speaks Marathi. Maratha = warrior name = sounds English/royal. Kesari = lion (Marathi). Lion roars in Marathi. Warrior writes in English.

🔥 Hook — 3 Delhi Durbars

Remember: 1877 (Lytton) → 1903 (Curzon) → 1911 (Hardinge II). Each is approximately 26-28 years apart. Or: L-C-H = "Lord C Has" → Lytton, Curzon, Hardinge.

⭐ Hook — Alipore Bomb Case People

Remember 4 names with 4 fates:
→ Khudiram Bose = Hanged (K for Killed)
→ Prafulla Chaki = Shot himself (P for Personal bullet)
→ Aurobindo Ghosh = Acquitted → went to Pondicherry for spiritual journey
→ Kanai Lal Dutta = Destroyed evidence (K = Kaafi clever)

➕ Hook — Ghadar Party Founders

"Lala Sohan Bhai Parmanand Kartar" → Lala Hardayal + Sohan Singh Bhakna + Barkatullah + Bhai Parmanand + Kartar Singh. Picture 5 people on a ship in San Francisco harbour flying the Ghadar flag.

Section 06

⚠️ Exam Traps

TRAP 1 — Alipore vs Maniktola

Both are names for the SAME 1908 bomb case. Alipore = where the trial happened. Maniktola = where the bomb factory was. Both names are correct. Don't pick one as wrong.

TRAP 2 — Khudiram Bose vs Aurobindo Ghosh

In a recent RRB NTPC paper, the "guilty" in Alipore Bomb Case was listed as Aurobindo — but he was ACQUITTED. Khudiram Bose was hanged. Aurobindo was released. Examiners will try to flip these. Always remember: Aurobindo → spiritual journey after acquittal.

TRAP 3 — Partition Announced vs Enforced

Announced: July 1905. Enforced/implemented: October 1905. Two different dates. Exam will give one and ask about the other.

TRAP 4 — Home Rule League Founders Swapped

Tilak's in Pune (April 1916), Annie Besant's in Madras (September 1916). Students often swap cities. Also: Madras league had MORE branches. Tilak's league was formed FIRST.

TRAP 5 — Muslim League Founded: Dhaka or Delhi?

Founded in Dhaka (present Bangladesh) in 1906. NOT Delhi. By Aga Khan + Nawab Salimullah.

TRAP 6 — Mitra Mela vs Abhinav Bharat Society

Mitra Mela (1899) came first. Abhinav Bharat Society (1904) formed later by V.D. Savarkar. Mitra Mela was then merged INTO Abhinav Bharat. Don't say Abhinav Bharat came first.

TRAP 7 — Komagata Maru Ship Nationality

It was a Japanese ship (NOT Indian). It carried Indian passengers. Destination was Vancouver, Canada — where they were turned back.

TRAP 8 — India House Location

India House was in London, NOT India. Founded by Shyamji Krishna Verma in 1905.

TRAP 9 — Anushilan Samiti Founders

Multiple Anushilan Samitis existed in Bengal. Bengal (1902): Satish Chandra Basu + Jatindranath Banerjee + Varindra Kumar Ghosh. Patna branch: Sachin Sanyal. Don't mix them.

TRAP 10 — Surat Session Presided by Whom?

1907 Surat Session was presided by Raj Ras Bihari Ghosh — NOT Tilak or Gokhale. Examiners love this.

Statement-Based & Assertion-Reason Patterns

A: Bengal was partitioned in 1905 primarily for administrative reasons.
R: Bengal was the nerve centre of nationalist activities and the British wanted to weaken it.
→ A is incorrect (it's the official reason, not the real one). R is correct. This is a classic Assertion-Reason trap.

Statement: Aurobindo Ghosh was convicted in the Alipore Bomb Case.
→ FALSE. He was acquitted. Khudiram Bose was hanged.
