Uttarakhand — First State to Implement UCC
🔥 High
  • Uttarakhand implemented Uniform Civil Code (UCC) Ordinance 2026, amending the UCC Act 2024
  • First Indian state to implement UCC after Independence through a specific Act
  • Goa had a colonial-era civil code (pre-Independence), so Uttarakhand is the first post-Independence
  • UCC applies to: marriage, divorce, inheritance, live-in relationships — for all residents EXCEPT Scheduled Tribes
  • UCC of Uttarakhand Act 2024 contains 392 sections

🔬 Science & Technology

Space missions, supercomputers, and scientific milestones.

PSLV-C62 Mission — Failed
🔥 High
  • PSLV-C62 failed during the 3rd stage (PS3 stage)
  • Primary payload: Anveshaa (Earth Observation Satellite N1) — lost
  • PSLV = Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle, called "Workhorse" of ISRO
  • Notable PSLV achievements: launched Chandrayaan-1, MOM (2013), Aditya-L1; set world record in 2017 by launching 104 satellites in a single mission
  • PSLV-C61 had also failed in 2025 — two consecutive failures
ISRO · Space
Aditya-L1 — Solar Storm Research
🔥 High
  • Aditya-L1 decoded the impact of a powerful solar storm on Earth's invisible magnetic shield
  • India's first space-based solar observatory
  • Launched using PSLV-C57 on 6 January 2024
  • Positioned at L1 Lagrange point in a halo orbit around the Sun
  • L1 is 1.5 million km from Earth
  • There are 5 Lagrange points (L1–L5) where gravitational forces balance out
ISRO · Solar Mission
Sunita Williams — NASA Retirement
🔥 High
  • NASA astronaut Sunita Williams retired after 27+ years of service
  • Her last mission (2024–25) involved Boeing Starliner; she commanded Expedition 72
  • Father was a Gujarati Indian; she took samosas to space
NASA · Space
Supercomputer PARAM Shakti — IIT Madras
Standard
  • MeitY launched supercomputer PARAM Shakti at IIT Madras
  • India's first supercomputer: PARAM 8000
  • Father of Supercomputing in India: Vijay P. Bhatkar
  • Indian scientists used supercomputer simulation to explain the Mpemba Effect (warm water freezing faster than cold water)
Supercomputer · IIT
