🎯 How the educator says to study this

Don't rote-learn everything. Specific things must be memorized exactly — book titles & authors, award names & winners, exact scheme names. These repeat in exams.

Understand, don't cram, the rest — the "aim/purpose" of a scheme or mission, or minor percentage figures, just needs a general sense; if it comes up you'll recall it from context rather than a memorized line.

Appointments: a name tied to a well-known body (CJI, NALSA Chairman) — memorize firmly. Lesser-known one-line appointments — just skim once, recognition is enough.

32nd Northern Zonal Council Meeting

🔥 High Priority
  • Chaired by Home Minister Amit Shah in Faridabad
  • Zonal Councils are statutory bodies, formed under the States Reorganisation Act, 1956
  • Total 5 Zonal Councils: Northern, Southern, Eastern, Western, Central
  • North East Zonal Council is separate — under its own 1971 Act
  • Chairman of Zonal Council = Home Minister
Compare — Inter-State Council: based on Sarkaria Commission (1988) recommendation, Article 263, constituted by President for 5 years, Chairman = Prime Minister, members = all CMs + LGs of UTs without legislative assembly.

🔥 High Priority
  • PM Modi inaugurated Skyroot's Infinity Campus
  • Skyroot Aerospace = first Indian private company to launch a rocket to space (sub-orbital rocket Vikram-S)

ISRO's Upcoming Timeline

🔥 High Priority
  • Triple spacecraft production capacity within 3 years
  • Gaganyaan (first human spaceflight) — 2027
  • Indian astronauts on the Moon by 2040
  • Indian Space Station targeted for 2035 (first unit assembled by 2028)
  • Chandrayaan-4 / LUPEX mission — 2028, with JAXA (Japan) — lunar sample return
  • Shukrayaan (Venus) — by 2028
  • Mangalyaan-2 (Mars) — 2030
  • Mangalyaan-1/MOM launched 2013, via PSLV-C25

Samudrayaan Mission

  • India's first human deep-sea exploration mission
  • Submersible: Matsya 6000 — goes to ~6000m depth, carries 3 people
  • Developed by NIOT (National Institute of Ocean Technology), HQ Chennai
  • Expected around 2026 (started 2021); purpose: explore polymetallic nodules

CMS-03 / GSAT-7R launched

🔥 High Priority
  • India's heaviest communication satellite (~4,400 kg)
  • Launched via LVM3-M5 (its 5th operational flight), to GTO
  • Multiband; mainly for Naval communication network

Vikas Engine delivered to ISRO

  • First human-rated Vikas Engine, by Godrej Aerospace
  • Will power the L110 stage of the LVM3 rocket for Gaganyaan

Government Schemes

Educator's tip: scheme names, launch years and ministries need to be remembered — the exact "aim" wording usually doesn't.

LPG Import Deal (Ujjwala Yojana)

  • 1-year deal to import 2.2 million tonnes of LPG from the USA
  • Under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana — Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas
  • Launched 1 May 2016; Phase 2.0 in 2021
  • Target revised from 5 crore to 8 crore connections

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)

  • ₹924.33 crore foundation laid in J&K by Jitendra Singh
  • Launched 2000; 100% Central Sector Scheme
  • Nodal Ministry: Rural Development · currently in Phase III

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

🔥 High Priority
  • 21st installment released by PM Modi on 19 November
  • 100% Central Sector Scheme, launched 1 Feb 2019
  • ₹6,000/year in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000
  • Ministry: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Jan Aushadhi Pariyojana

  • Saved citizens nearly ₹400 crore in 11 years
  • Started as Jan Aushadhi Scheme (2008), revamped as PMBJP
  • Nodal: Dept. of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers
  • Tagline: "Jan Aushadhi — Seva bhi, Rozgar bhi"

Credit Guarantee Scheme for Exporters

  • Cabinet approved 100% credit guarantee coverage
  • Via National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company Limited

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) — expanded cover

  • Now also covers crop loss from wild animal attacks and paddy inundation
  • Central Sector Scheme, Agriculture Ministry, launched 2016
  • Insures against yield loss from natural calamities and pest/disease attacks
