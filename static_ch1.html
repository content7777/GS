Layer 1 — Big Picture
What Is Census Really About?
Census is a complete headcount of every person in India — done every 10 years. For SSC, it means memorising numbers attached to states, UTs, and districts across 6 dimensions: Population, Growth, Sex Ratio, Literacy, Density, Religion.
📅 Timeline of Censuses
YearEventWho/What
1820Census of Dhaka cityBy Henry Walter — called Father of Indian Census
1872First census attemptNon-synchronized; during Lord Mayo's time
1881First complete / synchronized censusDuring Lord Ripon's tenure
1921Great Divide YearPopulation growth reversed — started booming after this
1961–71Highest population growth decadeFastest growth ever recorded in India
2011Latest completed censusLast census since independence — our main focus
2021Due but not conductedDelayed due to COVID + elections
🏛️ Who Conducts the Census?
Key Fact
The Office of the Registrar General and Census Commissioner conducts the census.

Current Census Commissioner: Mrityunjay Kumar Narayan (MK Narayan)
🔗 Connect: Lord Ripon's Legacy
Why Lord Ripon Matters
Ripon (only Viceroy given "Lord" prefix as respect):
✦ First complete census (1881)
✦ Father of Local Self Government
✦ Albert Bill controversy
✦ Hunter Education Commission
✦ First Factory Act
✦ Repealed the Vernacular Press Act

Memory: Ripon = Reformer with 6R gifts to India

L = Lakshadweep = Lowest UT — both in population AND it's the smallest UT. So when you think "smallest UT" → population lowest too. And because it's small and few people, its literacy rate is paradoxically very HIGH (91.8%). Small country = better metrics (like Nordic countries).

🏰 Memory Hook — Haveli People are Busy
Dadra & Nagar Haveli has the HIGHEST UT growth rate. Why? Because people in a Haveli (mansion) are always inviting more guests — the mansion grows! Meanwhile, Nagaland's population is shrinking because people are leaving (negative growth).
🗓️ Year of Great Divide — Why 1921?
Before 1921: famines, plague, low healthcare → population barely grew. After 1921: vaccines, improved food supply, healthcare → population skyrocketed. Like a dividing wall — before was slow, after was fast. 1921 = the starting gun of India's population explosion.

🧠 Memory: Daman = Oppression
Daman means "oppression/suppression" in Hindi. Fitting — Daman & Diu has the lowest sex ratio. Girls are suppressed there. Easy to remember: Daman → Damansuppressed girls → lowest sex ratio.
🧠 Haryana: Single Men State
Haryana has lowest sex ratio (879) AND lowest child sex ratio among states. Remember: In Haryana, boys far outnumber girls — that's why there's a famous social problem of young men staying single. Haryana = bottom of both sex ratio charts.
⚠️ Critical Trap — Ratio vs Child Ratio
Overall sex ratio INCREASED: 933 → 943 (good news ✅)
Child sex ratio DECREASED: 927 → 914 (bad news ❌)
These move in OPPOSITE directions — examiner will swap them!
State highest sex ratio = Kerala. State highest CHILD sex ratio = Mizoram (different!)

⚠️ Literacy Definition Trap
Literacy age threshold = 7 years and above (NOT 6, NOT 5, NOT 10)
Must be able to READ AND WRITE (not just one of the two)
Can be in ANY of the 22 languages in the 8th Schedule
Second-highest state literacy = Mizoram (SSC JE asked this!)
Second-lowest state literacy = Arunachal Pradesh (not Bihar)

🧠 Delhi vs Bihar — The Density Drama
Delhi's density (11,320) is 10 times more than Bihar (1,106). If you live in Delhi, you can feel this — it's terrifyingly crowded. Meanwhile, in Arunachal Pradesh (17/km²), you could walk for kilometres without seeing another person. Delhi = 10× Bihar. Bihar = 65× Arunachal.
🧠 A for A — Density Lows
Two A's have the lowest density:
Arunachal Pradesh = lowest state
Andaman & Nicobar = lowest UT
Both start with A. Both are remote. Both are mountains/islands with vast empty spaces.

⚠️ The % vs Absolute Number Confusion
Hindu population in ABSOLUTE NUMBERS increased (more Hindus exist in 2011 than 2001)
Hindu % of TOTAL population DECREASED (went from ~80.5% to 79.8%)
Both can be true at the same time — because total population grew faster than Hindu growth
Muslim absolute numbers AND percentage BOTH increased
Only ONE religion increased as a percentage share — Muslims
🧠 Remember the Two Growth Kings
In the growth rate race by religion:
Muslims lead with 24.6% — almost 1.5× the Hindu rate
Hindus at 16.8% — second highest but % share fell
After these two, everyone else (Christian 15.5%, Sikh 8.4%, Buddhist 6.1%, Jain 5.4%) had lower growth AND their share decreased.
Must know: Hindu (16.8%) and Muslim (24.6%) growth rates
⚠️ Exam Intelligence
Exam Traps — Where Students Lose Marks
These are the most common confusions that cost marks in SSC CGL, CHSL, and Steno exams. Read them once before every revision.
Group 1: Population Traps
Highest population state = UP. But highest 0–6 year population state = BIHAR. Two different answers!
Lowest population state = Sikkim. This remains same for both overall and 0–6 category.
India's population is 1.21 billion = 121 crore = 1210.2 million. All three are the same. Don't get confused by units.
17.5% of world population (not 17.64% — that's decadal growth rate). Easy number swap trap!
Group 2: Sex Ratio Traps
Overall sex ratio INCREASED (933→943). Child sex ratio DECREASED (927→914). Opposite directions!
Highest state sex ratio = Kerala. Highest state CHILD sex ratio = Mizoram. Different states!
Lowest sex ratio state = Haryana. Lowest child sex ratio = ALSO Haryana. Same here.
Highest UT sex ratio = Puducherry. NOT Kerala (Kerala is a state, not UT).
Child sex ratio options: 914 is the final census figure; 919 was the provisional census. If both appear, choose 914.
Group 3: Literacy Traps
Lowest literacy state = Bihar (61.8%). Second lowest = Arunachal Pradesh (NOT Rajasthan or UP).
Highest literacy UT = Lakshadweep (91.8%). NOT Kerala (Kerala is state, not UT).
Lowest literacy UT = Dadra & Nagar Haveli. The same UT has HIGHEST growth rate — opposites!
Highest literacy district = Serchhip (Mizoram). Lowest = Alirajpur (MP). Both unusual states — not the obvious ones.
Literacy definition: age 7+ (not 6+, not 10+). READ and WRITE (both required).
Group 4: Density Traps
Delhi's density = 11,320/km² (written with comma, NOT decimal point: 11,320 not 11.320)
Second highest density state = West Bengal (not Delhi — Delhi is UT)
Second lowest density UT = Mizoram (after Arunachal)
Andaman & Nicobar lowest UT density (~46/km²). Arunachal lowest STATE density (17/km²).
Group 5: Historical Traps
First census attempt = 1872 (Mayo's time). First COMPLETE/SYNCHRONIZED = 1881 (Ripon's time).
Henry Walter = conducted census of Dhaka CITY only (1820). NOT of all India.
Father of INDIAN Census = Henry Walter. Father of LOCAL SELF GOVERNMENT = Lord Ripon. Two different titles!
Year of Great Divide = 1921. Highest population growth DECADE = 1961–71. Two different facts!
Religion: only MUSLIMS saw increase in percentage share. Hindus increased in absolute numbers but % fell.
