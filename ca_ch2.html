🔥 High Priority

16th Finance Commission Report

  • Chairperson: Dr. Arvind Panagariya; report tabled in Parliament 1 Feb 2026
  • Recommendation period: 2026-27 to 2030-31
  • Vertical devolution: 41% share to states (same as 15th FC)
  • Horizontal devolution criteria weightage: Income distance, Population/Demographic performance, Area, Forest cover, and newly added — Contribution to GDP (replaces earlier Tax & Fiscal efforts parameter)
  • Income distance = difference between per capita GSDP of a state & average per capita GSDP of top 3 largest states; states with lower per capita GSDP get higher share (for equity)
  • Contribution to GDP = measured as average nominal GSDP between 2018-19 & 2023-24
  • Grants-in-aid recommended: ₹9.47 lakh crore over next 5 years (for urban/rural local bodies & disaster management)
  • Discontinued grants (recommended by 15th FC): Revenue Deficit Grant, Sector-specific Grant, State-specific Grant
  • Rural local bodies grant: ₹4.4 lakh crore; Urban local bodies grant: ₹3.6 lakh crore
  • Fiscal deficit target: Centre to bring down to 3.5% of GDP by 2030-31; States capped at 3% of GSDP
  • Combined debt of Centre+States to decline from 77.3% (2026) to 73.1% of GDP (2031)
  • Recommended privatization of electricity distribution companies (DISCOMs)
  • History: 1st FC Chairman - K.C. Neogy (1951); 14th FC - Y.V. Reddy; 15th FC - N.K. Singh; 16th FC - Arvind Panagariya
