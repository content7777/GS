Big Picture: India borrowed the Five Year Plan concept from Soviet Union (USSR). A Planning Commission (Chairman = PM) created 12 FYPs from 1951–2017, each with a target growth rate, focus sector, and model. NDA govt scrapped it in 2014 & replaced with NITI Aayog (est. 1 Jan 2015). The first 5 plans are 80% of exam questions. Master them first.
Origin Story: Concept → Soviet Union, Joseph Stalin, 1928 | India adopted it post-independence because of East India Company trauma → leaning socialist | Planning Commission Chairman = Prime Minister | Total 12 FYPs | Each had a "Perspective Plan" (20-year long-term vision) running alongside.
🏛️ Chapter Memory Palace

THE TRAIN OF INDIA — 12 Stations from 1951 to 2017

Imagine a long Indian train starting from Delhi station in 1951. Each coach is a Five Year Plan. You're the guard walking through coaches…

🚂 Coach 1 (1951-56): A farmer (Primary/Agriculture) is driving a HARE on a TAMER's leash (Harrod-Domar). Bhakra Nangal dam is the window view. Green fields. Target exceeded — farmer smiles.
🚂 Coach 2 (56-61): A big FACTORY appears. PC Mahalanobis is the engineer. 3 STEEL mills — Banana Rourkela🍌, Durga Durgapur🙏, Bhilai Chai☕. Rapid industrialisation.
🚂 Coach 3 (61-66): GAD GEEL (Gadgil formula) is the driver but the train gets hit — China 1962 hits from left, Pakistan 1965 hits from right. Two PMs die (Nehru, Shastri). Famine. Train barely moves.
🚂 ☕ HOLIDAY LOUNGE (66-69): Annual Plans. Chai break. New agricultural strategy. FCI, CACP, Green Revolution seed planted.
🚂 Coach 4 (69-74): INDIRA arrives. STABILITY + GROWTH. 14 banks get nationalised. Smiling Buddha 💣 nuclear test in Rajasthan (Pokhran).
🚂 Coach 5 (74-78): GARIBI HATAO sign on this coach. DHARMA model. RRB born (Moradabad, 1975). But Emergency (76) → Janata Party derails the coach at 78. Only 4 years.
🚂 🔄 ROLLING LOUNGE (78-80): Morarji Desai drives. ROLLING PLAN. 2 years. Then Congress returns.
🚂 Coach 6 (80-85): NABARD born (1982). Rural landless employment scheme. Literacy for 15-20 yr olds. Indira dies (Operation Blue Star, 1984).
🚂 Coach 7 (85-90): RAJIV drives. FWP = Food, Work, Productivity. Target 5, achieved 6. But 1990 crisis looms.
🚂 💸 CRISIS LOUNGE (91-92): Annual Plans. BOP crisis. Gold pledged. IMF bailout. LPG reforms born.
🚂 Coach 8 (92-97): PV Narasimha Rao + Manmohan Singh. Indicative planning. License Raj ENDS. Most successful. Target 5.6, achieved 6.8.
🚂 Coach 9 (97-02): VAJPAYEE. Social justice + equality. Nothing crazy to remember.
🚂 Coach 10 (02-07): Vajpayee then Manmohan. Horticulture Mission.
🚂 Coach 11 (07-12): MANMOHAN. Faster + Inclusive growth.
🚂 Coach 12 (12-17): MANMOHAN but NDA takes over in 2014 → train stopped. Faster + Inclusive + Sustainable → replaced by NITI Aayog (1 Jan 2015).

📜 Industrial Policy Resolutions (IPR) — Exam Favourite
3 Key IPRs: 1948 | 1956 | 1991

IPR 1956 (most important) — Came during 2nd FYP. Divided industries into 3 Schedules:
  • Schedule A (17 industries) — Exclusively Government (Atomic energy, Iron & Steel, Aviation, Railways etc.)
  • Schedule B (12 industries) — Govt primary, Private allowed with permission
  • Schedule C (remaining) — Private sector

License Raj: Started with IPR 1948 → Private companies needed govt license to set up industries → Ended in 1991 (LPG reforms). License Raj = corruption magnet. Its ending was key reform of 8th FYP era.

⚠ Exam Traps — What Students Get Wrong

⚠ Trap 1: Gadgil Formula Used in BOTH 3rd & 4th Plan

If question asks "which plan used Gadgil Formula?" — BOTH 3rd and 4th are valid. Look at other options to eliminate. 4th also had Rudra-Alan Model.

⚠ Trap 2: 5th FYP Was Only 4 Years (not 5)

Emergency 1976 → Janata Party came → ended 5th FYP in 1978 → introduced Rolling Plans. Most students assume all FYPs are 5 years.

⚠ Trap 3: Garibi Hatao = 5th FYP (NOT 4th)

"Garibi Hatao" slogan → Indira Gandhi → 5th FYP (1974-78). Students sometimes confuse it with 4th FYP. Hook: FIVE fingers → Garibi Hatao.

⚠ Trap 4: IITs (1st FYP) vs Steel Plants (2nd FYP)

IITs were set up during 1st FYP period. 3 Steel Plants (Rourkela, Durgapur, Bhilai) were set up during 2nd FYP. Don't mix these up.

⚠ Trap 5: Green Revolution Father

Global father: Norman Borlaug | Term coined by: William Gaud | India father: MS Swaminathan. These three are all separate — exam loves testing this.

⚠ Trap 6: NABARD vs RBI

RBI → REGULATES RRBs | NABARD → SUPERVISES RRBs. Different roles. NABARD set up 12 July 1982 on B. Sivaraman Committee recommendation. RRB established 1975 on Narsingh Mam Committee recommendation.

⚠ Trap 7: Smiling Buddha Location

Smiling Buddha nuclear test → Pokhran, RAJASTHAN (not Odisha, not Gujarat). Conducted on Buddha Jayanti. India = first non-P5 UNSC country to succeed.

⚠ Trap 8: Planning Commission vs NITI Aayog

Planning Commission Chairman = PM (like NITI Aayog). But Planning Commission was a statutory body; NITI Aayog is a think tank (non-statutory). NITI Aayog CEO = BVR Subrahmanyam. Vice Chairman = Suman Berry.

⚠ Trap 9: UTI → Axis Bank (NOT SBI)

UTI (Unit Trust of India) set up during 3rd FYP → evolved into what is now Axis Bank. Common confusion with other public sector banks.

⚠ Trap 10: PC Mahalanobis — Statistics Day

Indian Statistics Day = 29 June (PC Mahalanobis birthday). Don't confuse with 29 July (different day — check what's celebrated then).
